irrigation Drop System

As a part of my final project for EGR 102 (Found of Engineering Design Project), I created an Irrigation system for farms. The system can adjust the water level pressure according to the height of land by detecting through Ultrasonic distance sensor. The system promotes water conservation by avoiding overwatering and can be seamlessly integrated into existing irrigation setups, making it a versatile and sustainable solution for enhancing irrigation processes.

Sparkyville Energy Generator

The Sparkyville Energy Generation Project, created for the EGR 102 class, involved the development of a model that harnessed hydropower to generate electricity under specific conditions determined during the presentation. This project showcased the potential of hydropower as a sustainable energy source, with a customizable model representing the conversion of flowing water into clean electricity. By emphasizing adaptability, sustainability, and educational value, the project aimed to raise awareness about renewable energy solutions and promote a greener future.
